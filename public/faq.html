Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What is this app for?

This app is designed to manage HR-related tasks like attendance, leaves, payroll, and departmental information for employees.

Q2: Can I see my colleagues' info?

Yes, depending on your role, you can view employees in your department, their positions, and leave status.

Q3: How do I apply for leave?

Currently, leave applications are managed through the Leaves section in the app. Fill in the required details and submit for approval.

Q4: Is my personal information secure?

Yes, all personal data is stored securely and access is restricted based on user roles.

Q5: Are notifications supported?

Yes, notifications are provided for relevant actions like leave approvals, updates, and messages.
